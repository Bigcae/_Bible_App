# Short Summaries of Every Book in the Bible (KJV Overview)

> Back to [Documentation Hub](../../INDEX.md) | [Content Ideas](./)

This summary provides a brief, one-sentence overview of each book in the **King James Version (KJV)** Bible.

---

## **Old Testament**

### **Genesis**
The book of beginnings—creation, the fall, the flood, and God's covenant with Abraham.

### **Exodus**
God delivers Israel from Egypt and establishes His covenant through the Law given to Moses.

### **Leviticus**
God instructs Israel in holiness through laws for worship, offerings, and priestly duties.

### **Numbers**
Israel's wilderness journey marked by rebellion, judgment, and God's enduring faithfulness.

### **Deuteronomy**
Moses' final address, restating the Law and urging Israel to obey before entering Canaan.

### **Joshua**
Israel conquers Canaan under Joshua's leadership and divides the land among the tribes.

### **Judges**
Cycles of Israel's sin, oppression, repentance, and deliverance under God-appointed judges.

### **Ruth**
A Moabite woman's faith and loyalty lead her to become an ancestor of King David.

### **1 Samuel**
The rise of Samuel, Israel's first kings (Saul and David), and the transition to monarchy.

### **2 Samuel**
David's reign as king—his triumphs, sins, and the establishment of his dynasty.

### **1 Kings**
Solomon's reign and the division of the kingdom into Israel (north) and Judah (south).

### **2 Kings**
The history of divided kingdoms leading to Israel's and Judah's eventual captivity.

### **1 Chronicles**
A priestly retelling of Israel's history focusing on David's reign and temple preparations.

### **2 Chronicles**
The history of Judah's kings, emphasizing temple worship and covenant faithfulness.

### **Ezra**
The return from Babylonian exile and the rebuilding of the Temple in Jerusalem.

### **Nehemiah**
Nehemiah leads the rebuilding of Jerusalem's walls and the spiritual renewal of the people.

### **Esther**
God's providential preservation of the Jews through Queen Esther's courage.

### **Job**
A faithful man endures great suffering while wrestling with questions of God's justice.

### **Psalms**
A collection of songs and prayers expressing worship, lament, and praise to God.

### **Proverbs**
Wisdom sayings urging righteous living and reverence for God.

### **Ecclesiastes**
Solomon reflects on life's meaning and concludes that fearing God is true fulfillment.

### **Song of Solomon**
A poetic celebration of love between a bride and her bridegroom.

### **Isaiah**
Prophecies of judgment and redemption, including the coming of the Messiah.

### **Jeremiah**
God's warnings to Judah of impending exile, yet promises of a new covenant.

### **Lamentations**
Jeremiah's sorrowful poems over Jerusalem's destruction.

### **Ezekiel**
Visions and prophecies of judgment and restoration for Israel in exile.

### **Daniel**
Stories of faith in Babylon and visions of God's sovereign control over world kingdoms.

### **Hosea**
God's steadfast love portrayed through Hosea's marriage to an unfaithful wife.

### **Joel**
A call to repentance and prophecy of the outpouring of God's Spirit.

### **Amos**
God's judgment on Israel for social injustice and empty religion.

### **Obadiah**
A prophecy of Edom's downfall for opposing Israel.

### **Jonah**
A reluctant prophet learns God's mercy extends even to enemies.

### **Micah**
Judgment for sin and hope in the coming righteous ruler from Bethlehem.

### **Nahum**
Prophecy of Nineveh's destruction for its cruelty and pride.

### **Habakkuk**
A prophet's dialogue with God about justice and faith amid chaos.

### **Zephaniah**
Warnings of the coming Day of the Lord and promises of restoration.

### **Haggai**
A call to rebuild the Temple and renew devotion to God.

### **Zechariah**
Visions of encouragement for the returning exiles and prophecies of the Messiah.

### **Malachi**
A rebuke of Israel's unfaithfulness and prophecy of a coming messenger.

---

## **New Testament**

### **Matthew**
Jesus is revealed as the promised Messiah and King of the Jews.

### **Mark**
A fast-paced account showing Jesus as the powerful and suffering Son of God.

### **Luke**
The orderly Gospel highlighting Jesus' compassion for all people.

### **John**
Jesus is the eternal Son of God who brings life through faith in His name.

### **Acts**
The history of the early church and spread of the Gospel through the apostles.

### **Romans**
Paul explains salvation by faith and righteous living through God's grace.

### **1 Corinthians**
Paul addresses divisions, immorality, and worship issues in the Corinthian church.

### **2 Corinthians**
Paul defends his apostleship and encourages generous, faithful living.

### **Galatians**
A declaration of freedom from the Law through faith in Christ alone.

### **Ephesians**
The unity of believers in Christ and practical instructions for Christian living.

### **Philippians**
A letter of joy and encouragement to remain steadfast in Christ.

### **Colossians**
Christ's supremacy and sufficiency in all things.

### **1 Thessalonians**
Encouragement in faith and hope in Christ's return.

### **2 Thessalonians**
Clarification about the Lord's coming and exhortation to steadfastness.

### **1 Timothy**
Instructions for church leadership and godly conduct.

### **2 Timothy**
Paul's final letter urging faithfulness in ministry despite suffering.

### **Titus**
Guidance for appointing elders and promoting sound doctrine.

### **Philemon**
A plea for forgiveness and brotherly reconciliation.

### **Hebrews**
Christ's superiority as the perfect High Priest and mediator of a new covenant.

### **James**
Faith is proved genuine through righteous actions.

### **1 Peter**
Encouragement to endure suffering faithfully for Christ's sake.

### **2 Peter**
Warnings against false teachers and reminders of Christ's return.

### **1 John**
Assurance of salvation through love and obedience to Christ.

### **2 John**
A brief exhortation to walk in truth and love.

### **3 John**
Commendation of faithful service and warning against prideful opposition.

### **Jude**
A call to contend for the faith against false teachers.

### **Revelation**
A prophetic vision of Christ's ultimate victory and the new heaven and earth.

---

**Total Books:** 66
**Theme:** God's redemptive plan through Jesus Christ, from creation to eternity.
