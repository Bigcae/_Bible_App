# Major Women of the Bible (Ordered by Book)

> Back to [Documentation Hub](../../INDEX.md) | [Content Ideas](./)

This list highlights the **major women characters** in the Bible, arranged in canonical order by book, then by name.
Scripture references use the **King James Version (KJV)**.

---

## Old Testament

### **Genesis**
- **Eve** — Genesis 2–3
- **Sarah** — Genesis 12–23
- **Hagar** — Genesis 16; 21
- **Rebekah** — Genesis 24–27
- **Leah** — Genesis 29–30; 49:31
- **Rachel** — Genesis 29–35

### **Exodus**
- **Miriam** — Exodus 2:4–8; 15:20–21; Numbers 12

### **Judges**
- **Deborah** — Judges 4–5

### **Ruth**
- **Naomi** — Ruth 1–4
- **Ruth** — Ruth 1–4

### **1 Samuel**
- **Hannah** — 1 Samuel 1–2
- **Abigail** — 1 Samuel 25

### **2 Samuel**
- **Bathsheba** — 2 Samuel 11–12; 1 Kings 1–2

### **Esther**
- **Esther** — Esther 1–10

### **Job**
- **Job's wife** — Job 2:9–10

---

## New Testament

### **Matthew**
- **Mary (mother of Jesus)** — Matthew 1–2; 13:55
- **Mary Magdalene** — Matthew 27:56–61; 28:1–10

### **Mark**
- **Salome** — Mark 15:40; 16:1
- **Mary Magdalene** — Mark 15:40–47; 16:1–9

### **Luke**
- **Elizabeth** — Luke 1
- **Mary (mother of Jesus)** — Luke 1–2; 8:19–21
- **Joanna** — Luke 8:1–3; 24:10
- **Martha** — Luke 10:38–42
- **Mary of Bethany** — Luke 10:38–42 (by context with John 11–12)
- **Mary Magdalene** — Luke 8:2–3; 24:10

### **John**
- **Martha** — John 11–12
- **Mary of Bethany** — John 11–12
- **Mary Magdalene** — John 19:25; 20:1–18

### **Acts**
- **Priscilla** — Acts 18
- **Lydia** — Acts 16:14–15, 40

### **Romans**
- **Phoebe** — Romans 16:1–2

---

### **Summary**
- Approximate count of major women: **25**
- Many more women (named and unnamed) appear throughout Scripture, but these represent those with the most prominent roles in **redemptive and narrative history**.
